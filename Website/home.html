The KIWI RESTAURANT AND BAR is located in the histroical Grchamstown the heart of Thames township, also known as the gateway of the Coromandel Peninsula, North Island of New Zealand.

Our Restaurant is a family owned and operated restaurant offering a good variety of Kiwi & European Style Cuisine based on local and imported seafoods, steaks and wild food etc. We also provide special with modern trents.
